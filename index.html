ABOUT

Niagara Music Scene is a free, community-run live music calendar for the Niagara Region of Ontario. Every confirmed gig from Grimsby to Fort Erie — wineries, bars, legions, breweries, and festival stages — in one searchable place.

Built to replace the weekly Facebook post with something searchable, linkable, and always current. No login. No database. Just the music.
