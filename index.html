About this site

NIAGARA MUSIC SCENE

Niagara has a thriving live music community — but finding out who's playing where, and when, has always meant hunting through a dozen Facebook pages, venue websites, and word of mouth. Niagara Music Scene exists to fix that.

Every week, this site is updated with every confirmed gig across the region — from intimate winery sets in Niagara-on-the-Lake to festival stages in St. Catharines, legions in Welland, and bars along the Falls strip. No algorithm, no ads, no paywalls. Just the music.

The goal is simple: if you're an artist, you get more eyes on your shows. If you're a fan, you never miss a gig you would have loved. If you're a venue, your stage gets the visibility it deserves.

This is a community resource, built and maintained by people who care about live music in Niagara. Know of a gig we missed? Send it in — we'll add it.
